Making Cents of Money

A Financial Literacy Adventure for Middle Schoolers

How to Play

Each month, you receive $100 income. Distribute it between your accounts:

Chequing: For daily expenses. No growth but easily accessible.

TFSA: Tax-Free Savings Account. Grows 2-5% each month but has withdrawal penalties.

RRSP: Retirement Savings. Grows 3-6% each month but has withdrawal penalties and taxes.

Each round, a random scenario will affect your finances. Make smart choices to grow your wealth!

Warning: Consistently choosing the cheapest options may have negative long-term consequences!
